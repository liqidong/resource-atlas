# Public Redaction Policy

Resource pages may contain personal judgment. Before presenting a page as public-ready, check:

- `public_ready`
- `private_notes`
- `source_publishable`
- `redaction_needed`

Redact or rewrite:

- private local paths
- unpublished personal preferences
- long copied source excerpts
- sensitive toolchain details
- strong criticism that lacks evidence
- third-party copyrighted content beyond short evidence references

Public pages should keep opinion, but opinion must be tied to evidence.
