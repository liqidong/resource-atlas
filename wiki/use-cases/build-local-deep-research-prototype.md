# Build A Local Deep Research Prototype

Use this when the goal is to prototype a local-LLM-first research assistant.

## Best Current Resource

- [Local Deep Researcher](../resources/local-deep-researcher.md): strongest current entry for Ollama/LM Studio plus search-backed iterative research.

## Selection Notes

- Fastest option if you already have Ollama or LM Studio running.
- Best for learning the loop, not for final-answer trust.
- Choose SearXNG if the privacy boundary matters; DuckDuckGo, Tavily, Perplexity, and full-page fetches use external network services.
