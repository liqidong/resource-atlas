# Evaluate AI Trading Agent Risk

Use this when assessing whether an AI trading or investment-analysis agent is safe, honest, and evidence-backed.

## Current Resources

- [TradingAgents](../resources/tradingagents.md): useful risk case study because it combines LLM reasoning, market data, news, debate, and final BUY/HOLD/SELL proposals.

## Risk Questions

- Is the system clearly marked as research or advice?
- Are market data, news, LLM providers, and local model boundaries documented?
- Are backtests reproducible, and do they handle costs, data leakage, survivorship bias, and delayed data?
- Can prompt injection or untrusted news text influence final decisions?
- Is there any path from generated recommendation to real trade execution?
- Are forks, parent repos, releases, and stale model/API assumptions clearly labeled?

## Caveat

High community interest does not make a trading agent safe. Require stronger evidence than stars, demos, or a persuasive final report.
