# Compare Deep Research Tools

Use this when comparing hosted deep research products, open-source research agents, local-first prototypes, and browser/search agents.

## Current Resources

- [Local Deep Researcher](../resources/local-deep-researcher.md): local-LLM-first LangGraph template. Good for learning and prototyping. Not a mature product.

## Comparison Axes

- Setup cost
- Local/private boundary
- Search backend quality
- Browser or dynamic page support
- Citation quality
- Evaluation/benchmark evidence
- Ability to use private files or connectors
- Maintenance burden
